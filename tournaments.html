Poor Man's Smash Summit 2

Super Smash Bros. Melee Tournament

"Poor Man's Smash Summit 2" was the second edition of the Poor Man's Smash Summit tournament series. It was a local Super Smash Bros. Melee invitational tournament held in Houston, Texas from January 6th, 2016 by Ego Boost Gaming. Singles, doubles and side events were hosted. The tournament was held at the Summit gaming house, a private residence, and was not open to the general public.

Summary of events

The doubles tournament was first played in a 8 team round robin with the top 4 teams advancing to a single elimination bracket. Once the elimination bracket finished, niman and stork closed out a close 2-0 victory over fluke and STICK. The singles tournament was much anticipated as BLEND, a newcomer Falco main, competed in bracket. The pre-tournament hype lived up to it's name as niman edged out a close 2-1 victory over BLEND after changing from Peach to Dr. Mario after losing the first game. Hubsrico made a close 2-1 comeback against OUT when it was clear both players were not playing at their highest potential. Number 1 seed fluke then made a 3-1 victory over hubsrico even with a questionable character change to Marth on Final Destination which resulted in a 3-stock loss. Niman closed out stork in a nail-biting 5 game set where niman demonstrated his Dr. Mario is a force to be reckoned with against space animals. Niman made a upset against fluke as he won 3-1 with Peach to reach winners side of grand finals. In losers bracket, Burt beat BLEND with Samus, 2-1. OUT went on a tear in loser's bracket as he took out iMissHer in a 3-0 and then won a close last-stock set against stork with a great Marth combo. Burt lost a close set to Chef Ovenfire where he might be regretting his Ice Climbers switch on Yoshi's Story. Hubsrico made a reverse 3-0 comeback on Chef Ovenfire and then proceeded to buster out in a 3-0 against OUT. OUT's amazing loser's bracket run was ended in a quick 3-0 by fluke putting OUT out at a impressive 3rd place. Grand Finals pitted the top two seeds of the tournament with niman in winners side and fluke in loser's side. In anticlimactic fashion, fluke won both sets of grand finals 6-0 with niman playing Mr. Game&Watch in the last game.

Poor Man's Smash Summit Series

PMSS was created as an effort to produce a competitive esports environment amongst a group of friends that happened to all play Melee. Modeled after the actual Smash Summit series, it allows for the mediocre talent of the Ego Boost Gaming crew to shine and feel as if it means something.
