Game Library



Super Smash Bros. Melee

The most popular game in the Ego Boost Gaming library, Melee is largely the reason the organization exists and is a cornerstone of EBG community events such as the PMSS series as well as frequent smashfests and city-based tournament outings. Melee became a large part of the EBG group around late 2015, and only grew more popular from there.


Counter Strike: Global Offensive

A revered game among EBG members, CS:GO became popular among most members around early 2015, with many members achieving high ranks and playing constantly with one another. With the rise of Melee later in the year, many members took a step back from the game, but it is still revisited frequently as a comfortable, fun experience that never gets old.


Overwatch

Overwatch grew popular with EBG members mid-2016, and was arguably the overall most played game for the rest of that year. Due to changes within the meta, many players grew tired of the game and petered off in activity. The game is still frequently played by some members, and is still revisited every so often by others.


League of Legends

League was one of the first competitive video games for many members of Ego Boost Gaming, with many players have been playing since 2011. While not played as much anymore, some players, notably Spartan117, Checkmate, and Burt continue playing the game at a high level and stay updated with the game as it updates and progresses.


PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds

The newest addition to the Ego Boost Gaming library, PUBG has quickly become both a loved and hated game by many members. PUBG grew in popularity in the summer of 2017, and has maintained a frequent level of play among many members, most of which who aren't sure if they even like the game.


Rocket League

While not played by as many players on the roster, Rocket League has proven its staying power as a fun, competitive game that brings something new to the table with every game and update. An exciting game that often pits keyboard versus controller, Rocket League grew popular with most members in the summer of 2015, and is revisted occasionally by many members.
